凸凹・DEKOBOKO是一本獨立出版視覺藝術雜志。内容主要以插畫與漫畫為主,但當然不限制創作方式,將來非常歡迎更多不同形式的創作。目前由周和佳西兩人在2018年開始共同合作製作。 兩人即是創始人,同時又是漫畫插畫作者,編輯和發行人。 凸凹・DEKOBOKO is an independently published visual arts zine. We are primarily focused on illustartion and comics, but are steadfastly open to including other kinds of creative production. 凸凹is currently produced by Chou and Jiaxi, who serve as founders, cartoonists, illustrators, editors, and publishers.
